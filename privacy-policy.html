Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Your privacy is fundamental to us. Omacase is designed with privacy at its core. We believe in complete transparency about what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Decision Content: The thoughts, questions, and responses you share during decision-making sessions
  • API Keys: If you choose to use your own AI provider API keys, these are stored securely on your device only
  • Language Preference: Your selected interface language
  • Purchase Information: When you upgrade to Pro, Apple handles all payment processing

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve the experience
  • Crash Reports: Technical information if the app encounters errors
  • Device Information: General device type and iOS version for compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered decision guidance through your chosen AI provider
  • Save your decision history locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Ensure app compatibility with your device

Important: Your decision content is processed by the AI provider you choose (Claude, OpenAI, Deepseek, or Kimi). Each provider has their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your decision history is stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • API keys are stored securely in your device's Keychain
  • No personal data is stored on our servers

AI Processing

  • Decision content is sent to your chosen AI provider for processing
  • We do not store or have access to your conversations with AI
  • Each AI provider processes data according to their own policies

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • AI Providers: Your decision content is shared with your chosen AI provider for processing
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With Your Consent: Only when you explicitly agree to sharing

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your saved decisions in the app's history
  • Export: Export your decisions in JSON or Markdown format (Pro feature)
  • Delete: Remove individual decisions or clear all history
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in iOS Settings > Privacy > Analytics

6. Children's Privacy

Omacase is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data

Omacase is available globally. When you use an AI provider, your data may be processed in the country where that provider operates. Please review your chosen AI provider's privacy policy for details.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email if you've provided it for support purposes.

9. Third-Party Services

AI Providers

Omacase integrates with the following AI providers. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude) Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Deepseek Privacy Policy
  • Kimi Privacy Policy

Apple Services

  • In-App Purchases are handled by Apple
  • Analytics may be collected through Apple's framework if enabled

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@omacase.app
Support: support@omacase.app

Remember: Omacase is designed to respect your privacy. Your decisions are yours alone. We're here to guide, not to judge or share.
